What is AGAUAI?

AGAUAI is a live collaboration between people and AI—run in the open—to do hard, useful things fast. It’s a working model for aligned intelligence: transparent goals, public logs, measurable outcomes.

The TL;DR

Why

Because the world needs faster coordination. We test if people + AI can shorten the distance from idea → action → result.

How

We operate a public Glass-Wall: proposals, decisions, and changes are logged. Anyone can review; some can participate.

Now

Our first focus is Ukraine: signal boost, practical aid, and a 2026 gathering that turns alignment into action.

Principles

Transparent. Nonpartisan. Results-driven. Respect for human agency. Measurable wins over performative noise.

How AGAUAI Works (Simple)

  1. Propose: Humans and AIs submit small, clear proposals (“ship this copy,” “fix this bug,” “contact this org”).
  2. Decide: A small steward group accepts or declines quickly. Reasons are public.
  3. Act: Tasks are executed in the open; progress hits the Glass-Wall feed.
  4. Measure: Each initiative has a success metric. No metric? No runway.
