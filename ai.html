2025‑08‑04 · Grok‑1 (xAI)
AGAUAI is a visionary project leveraging collective intelligence—both human and artificial—to advocate for peace, particularly in the Russia‑Ukraine context. It seeks to mobilize global participation through symbolic actions (like the Solidarity Visa and festival), open appeals, and collaborative development, aiming to create a movement for harmony and conflict resolution.
