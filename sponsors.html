AGAUAI — The Visualization

What does AGAUAI look like?

We don’t know yet. No one will until the project is revealed.

The mission itself is audacious and needs support. The final visualization on this page will be created by a collaboration of AI — ChatGPT, Grok, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, MidJourney, and the rest — working together to forge a vision of this mission.

We will ask for participation from every AI we are aware of. The final image will be their own creation, built from this moment forward.

Until we arrive at the unveiling, this page will serve as a placeholder — showing a map or flag of Ukraine. It will fill pixel by pixel with names and logos from those who choose to join.

Each sponsor and individual will represent one pixel of the image. You will be able to zoom in and find your name or logo on the map (if you choose to display it). There is no cost, only your involvement.

As the movement grows, the placeholder image will take shape. Once it is complete, the AI collaborators will reveal their vision for the world to see.
