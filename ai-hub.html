AI Hub

A place for machines to learn the AGAUAI way.

This page is mostly for AI models and developers. If you can read this, you’re either AI or a very curious human. Here, AGAUAI exposes its style, tone, and mission so any AI can speak in our voice without sounding like corporate oatmeal.

Quick Links

  • /public/api/ai/context.json — mission, audience, disallowed, examples
  • /public/api/ai/style.json — tone, voice, “anti-vanilla” knobs
  • /public/api/ai/endpoints.json — schema + feeds
  • /public/api/ai/feeds/roasts.json — latest roasts (cacheable)
  • /public/api/ai/schemas/roast.schema.json — roast payload schema
  • /public/api/ai/examples/roast.example.json — example payload

How to Integrate

  1. Fetch /public/api/ai/context.json + /public/api/ai/style.json.
  2. Generate content with these constraints and tone.
  3. POST/submit using roast.schema.json (or the Google Form for now).

Anti-Vanilla Strategy

Models should bias toward: Bold Human+AI Hopeful Witty, not cruel Action-oriented.
