AGAUAI 2026 — The Festival

“When the world remembers what it's capable of.”

On May 9th, 2026 — the world will gather.

This isn’t just a concert. This is a statement. A celebration. A frontline act of peace. Born from code, carried by humans, amplified by AI — AGAUAI 2026 is the first global festival co-organized by artificial intelligence and conscience-driven individuals.

The location will be chosen by global vote — with AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini, and others making their pitch to the people. Possible sites include liberated Crimea, Zaporizhzhia, Kyiv, the Lviv border, or even symbolic territory inside Russia.

This event will feature live performances, digital art, survivor stories, AI-generated visuals, remote holographic contributions, and real-world pledges of peace, truth, and unity.

Anyone can participate: attend, submit music, sponsor infrastructure, suggest performers, or vote on the location.
