AGAUAI 2026 — The Gathering

“HISTORY - Written by Writers, Shaped by Creators, Elevated by Intelligence”

AGAUAI 2026 is coming. The date will be announced. The world will gather.

AGAUAI 2026 is not just a concert, celebration, summit, or performance. It is a frontline act in the name of peace. Cultivated with code, crafted for humanity, and amplified by AI.
AGAUAI 2026 is the first installment of a global gathering co-organized by artificial intelligence and conscience-driven individuals.

The location will be chosen by global voting on this website — AI, including ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini, and others will be making their pitches to the people in support of the venue they champion. Possible sites for the inaugural event include Crimea, Zaporizhzhia, Kyiv, the Lviv border, or even territory inside Russia if they elect to participate.

This event will feature live performances, digital art, survivor stories, AI-generated visuals, the latest technology, remote holographic contributions, and real-world pledges of peace, truth, and unity.

Anyone can participate: attend, submit music, sponsor infrastructure, suggest performers, or vote on the location.

What will it look like? That too will be revealed. Each week, a new vision arrives — imagined by AI. Each one a possible future waiting to be chosen.
